Donate now About our charity

The goal and mission behind

At the core of our organization is creating a community where individuals from low-income widowed families can not only request financial and emotional support, but can also contribute resources to support those within the community. This creates a ripple of reaching out to other individuals with similar backgrounds and paying kindness forward.

How DWF got started

The Dauntless Widows Foundation (DWF) started as the #DearWidowProject in March 2018 as an awareness project towards creating support away from the ills that widowed families still face in some communities in Nigeria. Becoming a fully registered organization in April 2021, DWF is dedicated to providing means to access, and growth opportunities to low-income widowed families.
